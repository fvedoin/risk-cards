Risk Control

Details

Risk Control involves monitoring risk mitigation strategies to minimize the impact of identified risks. During this activity, the project team takes proactive measures to control and monitor risks.

This can include regularly monitoring and assessing by implementing specific practices to manage the identified risks, tracking the progress of implemented mitigation practices, and adjusting them as needed.
