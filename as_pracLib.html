Practices Library

Details

This comprehensive compilation consists of a wide array of practices extracted from extensive literature, which were employed in some software projects. Each practice is accompanied by a brief descriptions and contextual information, providing a holistic understanding of their implementation.

Furthermore, for each practice included, there is a thorough examination of potential risks that may arise, as well as insightful accounts of past experiences related to their application. This comprehensive approach ensures that practitioners have access to a wealth of knowledge, enabling them to make informed decisions and mitigate potential challenges as they navigate their software development endeavors.

Supports:
  • Select practices
  • Allocate practices to cerimonies
  • Implement practices

Guidelines

  • The practices are available here .
  • On "Practices" page, you can see all the practices with a brief description and its source with more informations available.
  • On "Risks" page, there are some risks that were managed by the practices.
  • On "Previous experiences" page, you can find some caracteristics of previous projects which the practices were applied. The risk management processes that the practices were inserted are available as well. You should take this into account to decide if the practice fits your project.
  • On "Previous results" there are some comments about the previous applications. You can use this insights to decide if the previous implementations were successful.
