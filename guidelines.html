AGREED - Agile Risk Management Model

Guidelines

  1. Familiarize Yourself with the Cards: Before you dive into integrating the cards, take some time to familiarize yourself with their content. Each Essence Card encapsulates a specific aspect of risk management within an agile context. Understand the information and insights provided on each card to grasp their potential applications.
  2. Align Cards with Your Risk Management Process: Review your existing risk management process and identify key stages or activities where the Essence Cards can enhance your approach. Match each card to the relevant phase of your risk management lifecycle, whether it's risk identification, analysis, assessment, or control.
  3. Contextualize Agile Principles: One of the strengths of Essence Cards is their agility-focused approach. As you integrate the cards, ensure that you align their guidance with agile principles such as collaboration, adaptability, and continuous improvement. Tailor your risk management strategies to fit seamlessly into your agile projects.
  4. Use Cards for Decision-Making: When you encounter a specific risk-related decision point, consult the corresponding Essence Card. These cards provide actionable insights, strategies, and practical examples that can guide your decision-making process. By referencing the cards, you'll be equipped to make informed choices that align with your project's agile goals.
  5. Enhance Communication and Collaboration: Essence Cards also serve as communication tools. Share relevant cards with your team members during discussions, workshops, or agile ceremonies. The visual nature of the cards can facilitate clearer communication and foster collaboration among team members.
  6. Continuous Learning and Improvement: Integrate the Essence Cards into your team's learning culture. Regularly review and discuss the cards as part of retrospectives, knowledge-sharing sessions, or agile training initiatives. Encourage team members to contribute their insights and experiences, enriching the understanding of risk management within an agile context.
  7. Combine with Existing Tools: If you already have a well-defined risk management process/tool, Essence Cards can seamlessly complement your existing risk management tools, whether they are digital platforms or physical artifacts. You can incorporate the cards into your documentation or risk management software to enhance and reinforce agile-oriented risk management practices.
  8. Iterate and Adapt: Just as agile principles emphasize iteration and adaptation, be prepared to iterate your approach to integrating Essence Cards. Continuously assess the effectiveness of using the cards, gather feedback from your team, and make adjustments as needed to ensure optimal outcomes.
