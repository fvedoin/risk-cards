Nome do Modelo

Risk master

Their primary responsibility is to proactively identify risks and work closely with the team to develop appropriate mitigation strategies. They facilitate risk assessment sessions, encourage open communication, and maintain a risk register to track identified risks and their respective mitigation plans. Additionally, the Risk Master regularly communicates the status of risks to stakeholders, enabling informed decision-making and ensuring that risks are addressed in a timely and efficient manner.

Nome do Modelo

Team

The team's collective knowledge and expertise enable a comprehensive understanding of the project's potential risks, providing insights, and predicting their impact on various aspects, such as scope, schedule, and quality. By actively participating in risk management, the team helps create a culture of accountability, transparency, and proactiveness, ensuring that risks are effectively managed and the project's success is maximized.
